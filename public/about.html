About Me

Hello, my name is Kyle and I'm having a bit of a existential crisis on what I should do next with my career, so I have recently given myself the task of learning how to become a developer. May God have mercy on all of our souls.

I came to Australia over 10 years ago on a two week trip that never ended (apart from a hiatus during COVID). I have always loved living in Oz, and have been lucky enough to get to the country at a time that ecommerce was really starting to take off.

Within the last 10 months I joined Klaviyo; it's a terrific company with a top notch product...however there are some areas with the business that have really troubled me, and it has really made me sick of being an expendable employee. I want to start giving myself the opportunity to create what I'd like and allow the market to be my ultimate boss. Here goes nothing....
